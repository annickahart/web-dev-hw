Pesto Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of uncooked pasta of your choice
  • 1 cup pesto
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 8 oz. fresh mozzarella pearls
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • optional: olive oil

Directions

  1. cook the pasta according to the package, make sure to add salt to the water
    • once cooked, drain all excess water
  2. place pasta in a large bowl and stir in pesto.
    • fold in the mozzarella and tomatoes
    • season to taste with salt and pepper
    • add olive oil if mixture seems dru
  3. cover and refrigerate until ready to serve
    • stir before serving
